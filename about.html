About Us

Established in 1961, Bandra Urdu High School & Junior College is a community-centred institution delivering academic excellence.

Our Mission & Legacy

Education for All

Founded in 1961, Bandra Urdu High School & Junior College began as an Urdu-medium neighbourhood school and has expanded to offer Science, Commerce and vocational (MCVC) streams at the junior college level.

Our mission is to provide inclusive and affordable education that fosters multilingual skills (Urdu, English, Marathi), critical thinking, and civic responsibility. We are geared towards offering the best in class education and are equipped with technologically advanced educational facilities.

Facilities

We provide a conducive learning environment with:

  • Well-ventilated classrooms
  • Science labs (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • A computer lab
  • A library with Urdu/English/Marathi books (7850+ books)
  • Multipurpose hall for cultural events
  • Playground for sports

Our Faculty

We have a team of experienced state-board teachers with regular in-service training. The school leadership includes a principal and administrative staff who coordinate academics, examinations and student welfare programs.

Total Teachers: 47 (Male: 39, Female: 8)
