About Us

Welcome to Bandra Urdu High School & Junior College, Bandra West — a community-centred school established in 1961.

Est. 1961

Welcome to Bandra Urdu High School

We combine a strong foundation in Urdu language and literature with the Maharashtra State Board curriculum to prepare students for higher studies and meaningful careers.

Bandra Urdu High School & Junior College began as an Urdu-medium neighbourhood school and has expanded to offer Science, Commerce and vocational (MCVC) streams at the junior college level. Our mission is to provide inclusive and affordable education that fosters multilingual skills (Urdu, English, Marathi), critical thinking, and civic responsibility.
